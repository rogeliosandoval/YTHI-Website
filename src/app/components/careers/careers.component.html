Home Inspector in San Antonio, TX

Locally owned (non-franchise) multi-inspector home inspection service looking for full-time home inspectors. We serve home buyers and sellers throughout San Antonio and the surrounding area!

If you’re currently a solo inspector who’d like to do more inspections and not worry about answering the phone or missing calls, scheduling inspections, marketing your business, in other words – wearing all the hats – this is a solid opportunity with a friendly, professional company. Do what you do best and spend more time with your family. Or if you already work with a multi-inspector company and are looking for a different environment, this could be a great fit.

Professional back-office support, so your focus is outstanding customer service and inspecting. Pleasant work environment, competitive compensation, on-going training, equipment, and a friendly professional team.
